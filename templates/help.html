How to Add Items Through Django Admin Panel

1. Go to the admin panel and log in with your credentials.

2. Navigate to the section where items are listed, such as books or laptops.

3. Click on any section to access the dashboard.

4. In the dashboard, you will find fields like image_url, title, and price.

5. Go to Amazon or Flipkart, select the product you want to add, and copy the image link of the product. Paste it into the image_url field.

6. Give a title to your item.

7. Enter the price of your item.

8. Right-click and select the option to add a link. Go to Amazon, copy the link, and paste it into the appropriate field. Your product will be added successfully.
